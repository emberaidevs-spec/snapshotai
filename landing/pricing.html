Questions

Do I need an account to use SnapShotAI?

Yes, a free account is required to track your daily captures. Sign up takes 10 seconds — just email and password.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. You'll keep Pro features until the end of your billing period.

Is there a student discount?

Coming soon. We're working on .edu verification for discounted pricing.

What counts as a "capture"?

Each screenshot you analyze with AI counts as one capture. Taking a screenshot without analyzing it doesn't count.

Is my data private?

Yes. Screenshots are processed by AI and immediately discarded. We never store your images.
